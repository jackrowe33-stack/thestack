Everyone's skin, hair, and health are different. A product, ingredient, or routine that suits one person may cause irritation, an allergic reaction, or harm to another. Before starting, stopping, or changing any product or routine — particularly if you have a skin condition, allergy, sensitivity, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or take medication — **consult a qualified professional** such as a doctor, dermatologist, or pharmacist.

The AI assistant generates responses automatically and may be inaccurate or incomplete. Do not rely on it for any health decision. If you experience an adverse reaction to any product, **stop using it and seek professional advice.** If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your local emergency service immediately (in Australia, call 000).

By using The Stack, you acknowledge that you use it at your own discretion and risk, and that you are responsible for evaluating any information before acting on it.
